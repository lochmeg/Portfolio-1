About me:

Me:

Hello! I’m Lochlann Megannety, a student at IADT Dun Laoghaoire. I study graphic design and have been for the past three years. I enjoy the sytematic problem solving, the creative ideation and the combination of both.

Typography, motion and brand identity are definitely the fields which have peeked my interest. My personality and my process are quite similar, I would label myself a reductionist, I like to break things down into their component parts and build back an opinion, idea, concept or visual element that makes sense to me!
