Brand
Identity:

Problem:
Creating a brand identity for the 60th anniversary of the diplomatic relations between Ireland and Finland. Advertising an event taking place within the celebrations.

Solution:
The visual language follows the theme given by the client; past, present and future. The logo and graphics represent the past, taking influence from ancient irish design and traditional finish textiles. The typography is simple and modern, represnting the present. The colours are neon and bright, representing the future.
